## Why This File Exists

The model is worth studying for ORGANVM's own institutional design and for comparable alternatives.

## The Model (2007-2023)

- 56 fellows over 17 years
- ~1% acceptance rate (4 max per cohort, sometimes zero)
- **Salary equivalent** (negotiated individually) + **$275K/year project funding**
- **10:1 co-investment:** Fellow invests own money, Foundation matches 10x
- 30% equity stake for for-profit ventures
- 100% time commitment (but maintaining LLC/entity was fine)
- 1-year terms, renewable up to 3 years (max ~$825K project funding)
- Fellow retains all IP after fellowship
